# Roadmap

Kakunin is early and developed in the open. This roadmap is directional, not a
commitment — issues and PRs are welcome on any of it.

## Now
- Stabilize the public platform release; harden CI and supply-chain posture.
- Grow SDK coverage and framework integrations.

## Next
- OTLP / vendor-neutral observability export.
- Delegation chains (RFC 8693) and multi-region CA.
- US regulatory mapping alongside MiCA / EU AI Act.

## Exploring
- Opening more identity primitives (cert parsing, chain validation, CRL tooling)
  as community traction grows.

The hosted canonical certificate authority remains a service — see
[Open Source vs Hosted](https://www.kakunin.ai/open-source).
