# Kakunin — Identity Summary

**Company:** Kakunin  
**Founded:** 2026  
**Website:** https://kakunin.ai

## One-Line Description

Cryptographic identity and behavioral proof for AI agents in regulated industries.

## What It Is

Kakunin issues X.509 certificates to AI agents, monitors their behavior in real-time, and generates compliance reports for MiCA and EU AI Act audits. Every agent action is logged immutably, signed cryptographically, and auditable by regulators.

## Core Product

- **Agent Certificates** — X.509 identity tied to agent scope (e.g., max €50M transaction size)
- **Behavioral Monitoring** — 30-day rolling risk score; auto-revocation when agent drifts
- **Immutable Audit Logs** — WORM storage; every action timestamped + cryptographically signed
- **Compliance Reports** — PDF for regulators, JSON for pipelines
- **Public Verification** — Counterparties verify agent identity in <500ms (no account needed)

## Who It's For

Financial institutions, fintechs, healthcare systems, law firms, logistics, government agencies—anyone deploying autonomous AI agents in regulated domains.

## Key Problem Solved

AI agents in regulated industries face a blocker: **prove the agent stayed in scope and didn't misbehave.**

Kakunin solves it by binding identity, behavior, and proof at the infrastructure layer—making every agent action auditable and regulators able to verify independently.
